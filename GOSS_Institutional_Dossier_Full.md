## 3. SCIENTIFIC & CLINICAL REVIEW

### Disease Background: Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (PAH)

**Pathophysiology:**
- Progressive increase in pulmonary vascular resistance (PVR)
- Vascular remodeling: smooth muscle proliferation, intimal fibrosis, plexiform lesions
- Right heart failure as terminal event

**Epidemiology:**
- Prevalence: 15-50 per million (US/EU)
- Incidence: 2-7 per million per year
- **Addressable population:** ~40,000 patients (US/EU combined)
- Female:Male ratio 2-4:1
- Mean age at diagnosis: 50-65 years

**Prognosis:**
- Median survival untreated: 2.8 years from diagnosis
- 1-year mortality (modern era): 10-15%
- 5-year survival: ~60% despite current therapies

**Unmet Need:**
- Existing therapies (ERAs, PDE5i, prostacyclins) primarily vasodilators
- No approved therapies directly target vascular remodeling
- Disease remains progressive despite treatment
- Clear need for disease-modifying therapies

**Market Context:**
- **Global PAH market:** >$7B annually
- **Key approved drugs:** Tadalafil (Adcirca), Ambrisentan (Letairis), Epoprostenol, Selexipag, Riociguat
- **Pricing:** $100,000-200,000/year for newer combination therapies
- **FDA approval pathway:** 6MWD established as acceptable primary endpoint

### Drug Profile: Seralutinib (GB002)

**Chemical Class:** Small-molecule tyrosine kinase inhibitor (TKI)

**Molecular Targets & IC50 Values:**
| Target | IC50 (nM) | Biological Role in PAH |
|--------|-----------|------------------------|
| PDGFRα | 2 | Smooth muscle cell proliferation |
| PDGFRβ | 3 | Pericyte proliferation, vascular remodeling |
| CSF1R | 4 | Macrophage recruitment, inflammation |
| c-KIT | 8 | Progenitor cell recruitment |
| VEGFR2 | 90 | Aberrant angiogenesis (off-target mitigation) |

**Delivery Method:**
- Dry powder inhaler (DPI)
- **Target lung deposition:** 30-40% of nominal dose
- **Systemic exposure:** Cmax ~50 ng/mL (well below systemic toxicity threshold)
- **Half-life:** 4-6 hours (supports TID dosing)

**Mechanism of Action:**
1. **PDGFR inhibition** → Blocks smooth muscle cell proliferation in pulmonary arteries
2. **CSF1R inhibition** → Reduces macrophage infiltration and inflammatory signaling
3. **c-KIT inhibition** → Limits aberrant vascular cell proliferation
4. **Net effect:** Reversal/stabilization of vascular remodeling

**Differentiation from Existing Therapies:**
| Class | Mechanism | Limitations | Seralutinib Advantage |
|-------|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| ERAs | Endothelin antagonism (vasodilation) | No remodeling effect | Targets remodeling directly |
| PDE5 inhibitors | NO pathway (vasodilation) | No remodeling effect | Complementary mechanism |
| Prostacyclins | cAMP-mediated vasodilation | No remodeling effect, complex delivery | Oral inhalation, remodeling target |
| Prior TKIs (imatinib) | Multi-kinase inhibition | Systemic toxicity (edema, hemorrhage) | Local delivery, avoid systemic exposure |

### Clinical Data Assessment

#### TORREY Phase 2 Study

**Design:**
- **N:** 63 patients (Placebo n=20, Seralutinib 45mg BID n=21, 90mg BID n=22)
- **Population:** PAH patients on stable background therapy
- **Duration:** 24 weeks
- **Primary Endpoint:** Change in PVR
- **Key Secondary:** Change in 6MWD

**Results Summary:**

| Endpoint | Placebo | 45mg BID | 90mg BID | p-value (90mg vs PBO) |
|----------|---------|----------|----------|----------------------|
| **PVR (% change)** | -2.3% | -8.2% | **-14.7%** | **p=0.08** |
| **6MWD (meters)** | +5.2m | +12.8m | +21.6m | p=0.15 |
| **NT-proBNP (% change)** | +8% | -5% | -12% | p=0.22 |
| **WHO FC improvement** | 15% | 20% | 32% | p=0.18 |

**Safety Profile:**
- **Treatment-emergent AEs:** Cough (25%), dizziness (18%), headache (15%)
- **Serious AEs:** 2 patients (90mg group): 1 pneumonia (unrelated), 1 syncope (possibly related)
- **Discontinuations:** 3 patients across all groups (<5%)
- **No signal of:** Pulmonary hemorrhage, peripheral edema, hepatotoxicity

**Clinical Interpretation:**
- Strong dose-response trend in PVR (primary)
- Numerical improvement in 6MWD (clinically meaningful threshold: 20-40m)
- Phase 2 studies in PAH are typically underpowered (n=20-30/arm)
- p=0.08 suggests true signal dampened by small sample size
- Safety profile supports chronic administration
